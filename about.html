Our Mission

To democratize AI education across Africa by providing accessible, culturally-relevant learning opportunities that empower individuals and communities to harness the potential of artificial intelligence.

Our Vision

An Africa where everyone has the knowledge, skills and confidence to participate in the AI revolution.

Our Story

2025

Idea Spark

Our founders came together with a shared vision to bridge the AI knowledge gap across Africa, recognizing the transformative potential of AI education for the continent.

2025 Q2

Pilot Course

We launched our beta AI Foundations course, providing essential AI literacy to the first cohort of learners across five African countries.

2025 Q3

Ambassador Network

We recruited our first regional champions to help spread AI literacy and build local communities of practice across the continent.

2026

Scale Up

Expanded our reach through strategic partnerships and launched specialized bootcamps focused on industry-specific AI applications.

Core Values

Empowerment

Equipping individuals with the knowledge and skills to harness AI's potential for personal and community advancement.

Inclusion

Ensuring AI education is accessible to everyone across cultures, languages, genders, and economic backgrounds.

Innovation

Fostering creative approaches to AI learning and application that address unique African challenges.

Integrity

Promoting ethical AI practices and responsible technology use that respects privacy, fairness, and human dignity.
